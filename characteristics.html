German Shorthaired Pointer Traits

  • Physical Traits
    • Males - 23-25 inches.
    • Females - 21-23 inches.
    • Males - 55-70 pounds.
    • Females - 45-60 pounds.
    • Life expectancy is 10-12 years.
    • Smooth short coat.
    • Medium shedding.
    • Minimal to low drooling.
  • Personality
    • Extremely affectionate.
    • Good with children.
    • Open to strangers.
    • Good with other dogs.
    • Extremely protective.
    • Extremely playful
    • Extremely adabptable.
    • Extremely trainable.
    • High energy levels.
    • Needs a job or activity.
    • Medium barking.
  • Colors

    • Liver Roan
    • White & Liver
    • Black & White
    • Black
    • Black Roan
