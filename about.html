Documentation of the Development Process

The assignment for this website is to be a website developer that was approached by their uncle for help in developing a website for his small business. He asked for a visually appealing website that will need to have tables, forms, and a variety of multimedia elements. There will be five pages in total which include a home page and about page.
My assignment is for Uncle Tobias, a German Shorthaired Pointer fanatic who spends his entire life dedicated to these types of dogs. He wishes to share his passion for the breed with the world and create a platform to sell his handmade items such as bandanas, toys, and photo prints.
The steps included figuring out which elements would appeal to the customers that would likely view this website. Rough sketches of each page’s layout were made for each page’s baseline. Each page was then created and analyzed. After all pages were created, links and elements were checked. Then a thorough check of the rubric was made to ensure each assignment object was met.
